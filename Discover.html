Underground River

One of the best tourist spots to visit would be the Puerto Princesa Underground River. This place holds a spot as one of the 7 Wonders of Nature in UNESCO World Heritage Sites since 1999. It features a breathtaking limestone karst landscape with an underground river in a cave system with its natural rock formations that is directly connected to the sea.

Nacpan Beach

Its blue beach waters are so clear with no limestone rocks, seaweed gardens, and corals to be seen from a distance from the shoreline that may get in the way from a nice swimming experience. Away from urban areas would make you feel the peace and quiet like having the beach for yourself is surely relaxing. This beach also offers a nice sightseeing sailing and snorkel tour to enjoy the beaches. Definitely a must visit when looking for a island getaway to relax from overcrowded places to enjoy the beach, views, and breeze.

Considered to be the best place to visit in Coron would be its untainted crystal-clear freshwater Kayagan Lake. It is said to be the cleanest lake in all of Philippines allowing people to see underwater around 10 meters deep. This place will give you a breath of fresh air with its surrounding nature as well as magnificent rock formations surrounding the area. You may enjoy swimming in these clear waters and even try out their freediving activities. Why only stay on the surface looking at its clear waters when you can also go snorkeling and check out the underwater cliffs up close.
