Kayagan Lake being as the cleanest lake in the Philippines, it is one of the scenic spot in the island of Coron from docking area at the Kayangan Lagoon, the view is already mesmerizing. A 207 steps up and 174 down more or less has to be taken in order to reach the lake. The corals, mountains, and just the sea itself was definitely God's best creation! The view of the lagoon is best viewed at the view deck upon going up where it is really breathtaking from up there. It was certainly well preserved by a tribe that's currently residing there who are very welcoming to show there pride to the world. Come and visit Coron definitely a trip worth your time and money!

4.9             
A 30 - 45 min ride going downtown of El Nido lies a beautiful place called the Nacpan Beach. It is worth the hassle to go through the long ride because of how clear the blue skies are and the warmth of the sun allowing us to enjoy the beautiful beach and its blue hues. Hopping in and out of the beach, jumping through waves, floating on the surface while waves crash on our bodies is life's pure joy. There are also nice restaurants nearby with lots of different foods. We also received on-beach massages while witnessing a beautiful sunset, which were wonderful. Surely worth paying a visit as one of the best beaches to find at El Nido.
